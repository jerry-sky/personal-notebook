CSS — ungroup content

Use display: contents; to make element structurally transparent, to ungroup its children. The following structure

<div class="page-header">
  <h1>Recent articles</h1>
  <p>A look at my recent articles about CSS</p>
</div>

after applying

.page-header {
  display: contents;
}

functionally becomes this:

<!-- <div class="page-header"> -->
<h1>Recent articles</h1>
<p>A look at my recent articles about CSS</p>
<!-- </div> -->
